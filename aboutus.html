Established: 2012

WVO Barbell Club

The gym portion of Walnut Valley Outpost began in 2012. It is located in the basement of 107 E. 7th Ave. in Winfield, KS! Our training methodology includes interval style training with lifting, running, pull-ups and a litle bit of everything in between.

WVO Bike Shop

At 702 Main St., right next to our basement gym, you'll find our bike shop. Here, we offer sales, repairs, and a variety of bike accessories. Additionally, we organize county-wide bike races, with the main event being Elrods in May every year!
